%\section{S1}

The Connect List (Participant List) shows the participants of the meeting; in case of a COVISE collaborative session these are the
user who started the session as "Master" and all users that have subsequently been added by an "AddPartner".

The list controls the state and the rights of the participants. 

The services are provided by "Sametime Connect", so you can look for detailed information in the Sametime User's
Guide (see Usage Hints).
