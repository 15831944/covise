By pressing the Projection Mode button you can switch between

  • perspective and
  • orthogonal projection

    • (default: perspective)
Note:
The choice of the projection mode changes name and function of the Dolly/Zoom Wheel (see Wheels)
  • perspective projection: Dolly wheel

    • (physical distance of the camera is changed like moving the camera on a dolly)
  • orthogonal projection: Zoom wheel

    • (focussing distance of the camera is changed like using a zoom lens)

The options ViewAll, SeekMode and ProjectionMode change your view of the object(s) in the viewer area.
