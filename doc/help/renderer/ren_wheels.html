Wheels

Note:
Wheels (plus menu bar, information area, and tool bar) will disappear if you set 'Decoration off' in the Viewer Popup Menu  (menu Covise Renderer)

In addition to the mouse operations via tool bar you can change the viewpoint by three thumb Wheels at the left side, at the bottom, and at the right side of the viewer area.
 

  • Rotx

    • (left side)
    Rotate the scene around the x-axis
     
  • Roty

    • (bottom)
    Rotate the scene around the y-axis
    Note:
    You can 'show point of rotation axes' using the corresponding option of the  PreferenceSheet.
  • Dolly/Zoom

    • (right side)
    Name and function of this wheel depend on the ProjectionMode:
    • perspective projection: Dolly -  physical distance of the camera is changed like moving the camera on a dolly
    • orthogonal projection: Zoom - focussing distance of the camera is changed like using a zoom lens
    Note:
    You can change range and start value defaults of your 'zoom factor' by using the PreferenceSheet.
