Navigation - "ViewAll" (VIEW_ALL)

 
  • The whole scene is scaled to fit into the visible world

    •  
  • The size of the visible world is as specified in covise.config:


    • COVERConfig
    {
            SCENESIZE <size [mm]>
    }
     

  • The optimal size for the visible world is the (smaller) size of the screen

    •  
  • The center of scaling is the center of a sphere around the scene (bounding box)
