Navigation - "Move World" (XFORM)

 
  • Moves (translates and rotates) the whole scene

    •  
  • Interaction:

    •     -   Place hand/icon into the scene you want to move,
        -   press button, keep button pressed and move your hand, release button
     
  • Movement is done relative to your hand, your hand is the center of rotation

    •  
  • Shortcut for 3-button-device: right button
