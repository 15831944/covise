Stereo - optimal positioning of objects

 
  • Long distance objects don‘t look like 3D

    •  
  • Very close objects look duplicate

    •  
  • Try to get zero-parallax on the screen, because there is the focus of your eyes

    • Optimal position for small objects: close to the screen, one half behind, one half in front of the screen
     
  • Optimal position for large scenes:

    •     -   either a big screen
        -   or objects must be placed behind the screen (screen = window)
