Stereo - active/passive

 
  • Active Stereo:
        -   2 images in separate video storages
        -   special video format
        -   shutter  glasses
     
     

     
  • Passive Stereo:
        -   2 images in separate windows
        -   Graphic card with 2 video outputs
        -   2 projectors with polarisation filters
        -   glasses with polarisation filter
