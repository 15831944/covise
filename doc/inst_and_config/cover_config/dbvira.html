COVER Configuration for the DBVira

Transmitter

The Transmitter is mounted behind the front wall with x-axis up and y-axis right, z axis in (cable down and the mounting wholes NOT used).

Receiver

The Receiver is mounted in the left side of the stereo glasses. The cable goes down.

Stylus

used for hand

Screen

Workbench Screen, size is 1460 x 1100 mm. The resolution is 1024x768 in 96 Hz Stereo. Screen angle 50 degree.
