COVER Configuration for the Workbench of the British Aerospace Sowerby Research Center

Transmitter

The Transmitter is mounted on the front wall with x-axis up and y-axis right, z axis in (cable down and the mounting whole used for mounting it from aoutside to the wall).

Receiver

The Receiver is mounted in the right side of the stereo glasses. If the user stands in front of the workbench, x-axis is in, z axis is left, y axis is down.

Stylus

used for hand

Screen

Workbench Screen, size is 1800 x 1100 mm. The resolution is 1600x980 in 96 Hz Stereo. Screen angle 10 degree.
